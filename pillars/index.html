Our Thematic Pillars

SIC revolves around four thematic pillars designed to inspire and equip attendees for success in today's fast-paced world.

Social Change

Harness the power of innovative ideas and strategies to drive social impact. Learn how to create meaningful change in your community, tackle social challenges, and promote sustainability.

Technology and Innovation

Explore cutting-edge technological advancements and innovations that are shaping the future. From AI to blockchain, get insights into the latest trends and how they can be applied to solve real-world problems.

Business and Entrepreneurship

Whether you're starting out or scaling up, SIC offers sessions on business strategies, funding, marketing, and leadership. Learn from successful entrepreneurs and industry leaders.

Marketplace (Faith in the Workplace)

Discover how to integrate Christian principles into the workplace while maintaining excellence and professionalism. Marketplace offers unique sessions on faith-driven leadership and ethical business practices.

The Scale and Ideas Conference 2025 is a premier event focused on Social Change, Technology and Innovation, Business and Entrepreneurship, and Marketplace (Faith in the Workplace). Join us in shaping the future!
