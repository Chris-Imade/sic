ABOUT EVENT

Welcome to the Scale and Ideas Conference (SIC) 2025

The human mind may be likened to a garden, which may be intelligently cultivated or allowed to run wild. But whether cultivated or neglected, it must and will produce. At SIC, we plant useful seeds into your mind. As it falls, it will accumulate and then reproduce its kind. SIC focuses on four key pillars that shape our society and future: Social Change, Technology and Innovation, Business and Entrepreneurship, and Marketplace – connecting faith with workplace success. Join us for thought-provoking sessions, networking, and opportunities to scale your ideas.
  • Exclusive insights from industry leaders and innovators.
  • Opportunities to network with entrepreneurs and professionals.
  • Sessions focused on social change, technology, business, and faith-driven success.
  • Collaborative discussions to help scale your ideas and impact.
