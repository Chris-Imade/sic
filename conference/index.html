ABOUT EVENT

Welcome to the Scale and Ideas Technology Summit (SIT) 2026

Africa stands at a pivotal point in its digital evolution. With one of the fastest-growing youth populations and increasing mobile and internet penetration, the continent is poised to leapfrog traditional development trajectories through technology. SIT explores how African context, culture, and unique challenges create unprecedented opportunities for innovation. Join us as we examine technology through an authentically African lens, celebrating our heritage while building solutions for tomorrow.
  • Deep dive into African-centered technology solutions and innovations.
  • Networking with tech entrepreneurs, developers, and industry leaders.
  • Sessions on AI, blockchain, fintech, and emerging technologies in African contexts.
  • Workshops on building scalable tech solutions for African markets.
  • Discussions on digital transformation and technology policy in Africa.
