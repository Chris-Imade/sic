Scale and Ideas Technology Summit (SIT)

Technology Through the African Lens: Context, Culture, and Opportunity

March 27-28, 2026
Edo State, Nigeria
FREE Attendance

Africa stands at a pivotal point in its digital evolution. With one of the fastest-growing youth populations and increasing mobile and internet penetration, the continent is poised to leapfrog traditional development trajectories through technology.

Join us for an immersive exploration of technology's role in shaping Africa's future, where innovation meets cultural context and opportunity. This summit will convene technologists, founders, policy makers, development partners, educators, and ecosystem builders.
