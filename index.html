Scale and Ideas Podcast

Africa and its ideas, innovators, and impact builders are rising. Hosted by Izoduwa Precious Asemota, a global technology strategist, startup mentor, and ecosystem builder, Scale is where bold ideas meet real-world execution.

Each episode explores the parameters and what it takes to shape Africa's digital and entrepreneurial future. Whether you're a founder building from scratch or a young professional with a dream, this podcast will equip you with insights, tools, and inspiration to scale what matters—your businesses, your purpose, and your impact.

Meet Your Host

Izoduwa Precious Asemota

Global Technology Strategist, Startup Mentor, and Ecosystem Builder

Izoduwa Precious Asemota brings a wealth of experience in technology strategy and startup development to the SIC Podcast. As a respected ecosystem builder, Precious has helped numerous entrepreneurs navigate the complex landscape of scaling their ideas from concept to impactful businesses.

On Scale, Precious explores the cutting edge of African innovation, connecting listeners with the insights, tools, and inspiration needed to build successful ventures that address real challenges across the continent.
