ABOUT EVENT

Welcome to the Scale and Ideas Conference (SIC) 2025

Join a dynamic gathering of visionaries where forward-thinkers, change-makers, entrepreneurs, and professionals gather to create impactful social and business solutions. SIC focuses on four key pillars that shape our society and future: Social Change, Technology and Innovation, Business and Entrepreneurship, and Marketplace – connecting faith with workplace success. Join us for thought-provoking sessions, networking, and opportunities to scale your ideas."
  • Exclusive insights from industry leaders and innovators.
  • Opportunities to network with entrepreneurs and professionals.
  • Sessions focused on social change, technology, business, and faith-driven success.
  • Collaborative discussions to help scale your ideas and impact.
